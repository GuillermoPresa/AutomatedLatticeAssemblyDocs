\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pos_x}{PosX}} represents the Pose of a robot according to the cartesian space configuration of its tool. \begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Depending on the number of joints a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pos_x}{PosX}} may have more than one \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pos_j}{PosJ}} representation. In the case at hand a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pos_x}{PosX}} may have at most 2 \mbox{\hyperlink{class_pos_j}{PosJ}} representations
